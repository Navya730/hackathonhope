Women In Technology Challenge

Terms and Conditions

  • Teams must consist of a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 5 participants, of which at least one member must be a HopeWorks student.
  • Each team must ensure that at least 50% of its members are women.
  • The contest will be conducted in two phases. Phase 1 will be conducted virtually.
  • Phase 2 will be limited to the finalists shortlisted from Phase 1. The final round of Phase 2 will be conducted in person in Bengaluru in May 2026.
  • All registration and participant data will be handled in compliance with applicable government regulations and data confidentiality standards.
